<CaseCard label="Overview" index="01">

BisectHosting is a Minecraft and game-server host with a long-running modpack and community-creator program. I joined as a graphic designer in 2022 and spent the next 21 months on brand work, content design, and partner-program strategy that ran across the company's own channels and through its partners.

Three threads from that work are surfaced here: the v2.0 brand guidelines, the partner / press kits page, and the content design and strategy that helped partner projects succeed.

</CaseCard>

<CaseCard label="Brand Guidelines" index="02">

The guidelines document covered the v2.0 logo system, type, palette, surface treatments, and usage rules across product, marketing, and partner-facing surfaces. The aim was a small, enforceable system rather than a thick book of one-off examples, so anyone making a card, a tweet, or a sponsor read could compose on-brand assets without checking back in for each piece.

<Figure src="/BisectHosting-BrandingGuidelines.png" alt="BisectHosting brand guidelines document spread." />

</CaseCard>

<CaseCard label="Press and Creator Kits" index="03">

The partner-facing kits page packaged the brand for two audiences: press contacts who needed clean logo and screenshot assets on short notice, and content creators in the BisectHosting partner program who needed sponsor-read graphics, overlay templates, and palette references.

The same kit format scaled into the partner campaigns the company ran through 2022 and 2023, with assets I worked on ending up in programs at GamersOutreach, Twitch, Project Hope, and Tiltify.

<Figure src="/BisectHosting-Press-Kits.png" alt="BisectHosting press and creator kits page." />

</CaseCard>

<CaseCard label="Partner Projects" index="04">

A meaningful share of the role was content design and strategy — helping partner projects land. That meant working through identity, scene composition, promo assets, and channel strategy alongside the partner team, then packaging the result so it fit cleanly into how each project went to market on CurseForge, Twitch, and adjacent platforms.

The output spanned title-screen scenes, key art, and supporting content across a long list of modpacks and creator projects through 2022 and 2023. MCEternal 2 is one of those.

<Figure src="/MCEternal2.png" alt="One of the partner projects: MCEternal 2 title-screen scene." />

</CaseCard>

<CaseCard label="Outcome" index="05">

The 21-month run covered brand, motion, content, and strategy work across product launches, partner programs, and creator campaigns. It's also the product-design background that sits underneath every game project on this site — the muscle that makes the game-design work ship on time.

</CaseCard>
