<CaseCard label="My Role" index="02">

Game designer. Owned the core loop and kept it compatible with the narrative beats, since the experience is built around narrative puzzle-solving rather than action.

- Core gameplay loop. Carries the story without interrupting it.
- Puzzle mechanics and the probability system underneath.
- Lighting and optimisation across the house.
- Level design, shaping stages around the narrative.
- Technical art and shaders for the visual language.
