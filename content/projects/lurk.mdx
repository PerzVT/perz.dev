<CaseCard label="Overview" index="01">

Lurk is a single-player first-person horror game. The player explores a house they have inherited from the 1800s, uncovering the building's dark history while dealing with the paranormal phenomena that come with it.

With a small team, I had the chance to stretch beyond pure design work and contribute to level design, shaders, and technical art as the project needed it.

<CaseCard label="My Role" index="02">

As Game Designer, my focus was the core gameplay loop and keeping it compatible with the narrative beats, since the experience is built around narrative puzzle-solving rather than action.

- Designed the core gameplay loop so it could carry the story without interrupting it.
- Designed the puzzle mechanics and the probability system underneath them.
- Worked on lighting and optimization across the house.
- Assisted with level design and shaped game stages around the narrative.
- Contributed to technical art and shaders to support the visual language of the game.

</CaseCard>
