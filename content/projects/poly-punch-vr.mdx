VR rhythm game built on an experimental Meta hand-tracking package. Combat gestures without controllers, extending the kit's Beat-Saber-like prototype into a wider gesture-progression space.

A student build for our team's VR pipeline. Comfort, locomotion constraints, gesture readability under load.
