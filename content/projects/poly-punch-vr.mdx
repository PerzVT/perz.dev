<CaseCard label="Overview" index="01">

Poly Punch VR is a VR rhythm game that uses an experimental hand-tracking package from Meta to define combat gestures without controllers. We built on top of the kit's initial Beat-Saber-like ideas and expanded its design space around gesture variety and progression.

The project was a student build that let our team sharpen our VR pipeline and work through VR-first principles, from comfort and locomotion constraints to how readable a gesture needs to be under load.

</CaseCard>

<CaseCard label="My Role" index="02">

As Game Designer, I worked on the core gameplay loop and focused primarily on progression and the introduction of new gestures over time.

- Designed the core gameplay loop and progression curve.
- Designed new gesture unlocks and their reward distribution.
- Designed the score and combo system.
- Led level design, including whitebox and blockout, building spaces that taught the movement and gesture kit naturally instead of through tutorial pop-ups.
- Contributed to technical art and shaders to support the game's visual language.

</CaseCard>
