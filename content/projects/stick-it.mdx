Short stealth-action game built in seven days by a four-person team at Knite Studios. The player is a farmer named Pedro sneaking through cartel territory.

<CaseCard label="My Role" index="02">

Project manager and lead designer.

- Core stealth loop. Detection cones, noise propagation, distraction, cover rules.
- Guard AI across patrol, suspicion, and pursuit states.
- Progression. Each level introduced one new tool against a stable rule set.
- UI and HUD, including the detection-state grammar.
- Team coordination across art, programming, design, and UI under a hard deadline.

</CaseCard>

<CaseCard label="Outcome" index="03">

Shipped on itch.io by the jam deadline. Playable end-to-end.
